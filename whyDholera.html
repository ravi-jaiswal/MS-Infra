India's First Greenfield Smart City

  • Dholera is the first planned smart city developed from scratch.
  • It is a key project under the Delhi-Mumbai Industrial Corridor (DMIC).
  • The development is backed by both central and state governments.
  • This government support reduces risks and builds confidence in long-term development.

Massive Infrastructure Development

  • World-class infrastructure is under development.
  • 6-lane expressways are being built for seamless transport.
  • Monorail and metro connectivity planned for easy commuting.
  • International airport (Dholera International Airport) near completion.
  • Flood-free city with underground cabling, smart roads, and integrated city services.

High ROI Potential

  • Designated as a Special Investment Region (SIR).
  • Focused on attracting manufacturing industries, IT, electronics, defence, and aerospace companies.
  • Encouraging EV and green energy firms to set up operations.
  • Major companies like Reliance, Tata, and Vedanta are exploring investments.

Focus on Industrial and Technological Growth

  • Located along the Delhi-Mumbai Industrial Corridor (DMIC) for strong connectivity.
  • Only 100 km from Ahmedabad via the Ahmedabad-Dholera Expressway.
  • Close proximity to ports like Pipavav and Kandla for trade activities.

  • Investor-friendly policies to promote growth.
